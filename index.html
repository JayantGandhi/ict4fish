ICT4Fish

In recent years, stocks of pelagic fish in the Java Sea have decreased dramatically due to overfishing. This increased pressure on pelagic fish stocks is not just due to increase demand (Indonesia is one of the world's highest per capita consumer of fish), but also from wasteful and unsustainable fishing practices fueled by asymmetric market information and lack of fish stock management.

What is ICT4Fish?

ICT4Fish is an SMS based initiative to help curb overfishing by delivering accurate and real-time market information to small scale fishermen in the Java Sea.
